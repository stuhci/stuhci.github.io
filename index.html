Multimodal Human-Computer
Interaction Lab

We are a HCI research team at Shantou University, China. We are dedicating into combining Design and Technologies with Human-Centered Thinking to create intuitive and expressive interactive systems. Our research explores interactive design and technology through interdisciplinary researches including sensing technologies, gesture-based interaction, multimodal UI/UE for VR/AR, intelligent interfaces/tools, and empirical research of HCI.
我们是来自汕头大学的人机交互研究团队。我们致力于将设计和技术与以人为本的思维相结合，打造直观且富有表现力的交互系统。我们通过跨学科研究探索交互设计和技术，包括传感技术、手势交互、VR/AR的多模态用户界面、智能界面/工具，以及人机交互的实证研究。

We are actively looking for students/interns/scholars from different disciplines who have exceptional engineering or design thinking skills.
Please get in touch with Dr. Taizhou Chen (tzchen at stu dot edu dot cn) if you want to join us.

我们正在积极招募具有卓越工程能力或设计思维的跨学科研究生/本科生/学者。
如果你想加入我们，请与陈泰舟博士联系(tzchen at stu dot edu dot cn)。
